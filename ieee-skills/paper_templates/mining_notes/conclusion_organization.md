# Conclusion Organization

Publisher family: IEEE

Close the paper by restating contribution, evidence, and future trajectory without redoing the abstract.

## Fixed routines

### Contribution-evidence-impact

**Sequence:** problem revisited → method contribution → key evidence → broader technical implication → future direction

**Reviewer risk:** Conclusion that merely repeats results feels weak.

### Takeaway compression

**Sequence:** one-sentence core takeaway → two evidence anchors → one limitation/future bridge

**Reviewer risk:** Too many claims dilute the final message.

### No-new-claims rule

**Sequence:** only claims already supported → no new experiments → no new terminology → no unsupported application promise

**Reviewer risk:** New claims in conclusion can trigger reviewer skepticism.

## Output contract

- closing paragraph skeleton
- evidence anchors
- safe future-work sentence
- no-new-claim audit

## Safety note
These routines are structural best-practice patterns, not official publisher rules and not text to copy from template papers.

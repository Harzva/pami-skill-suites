Source Aging Policy

Sources are classified as fresh, aging, stale, or unchecked. Offline release builds may remain valid, but unchecked or stale official-source records must be reverified before real submission advice.

Offline-safe policy

Dry-run release builds must not mark unchecked sources as healthy. Mutable publication details require live verification before real submission advice.

# IEEE Style Notes and Cross-Family Differences

## IEEE writing default

- Lead with problem, gap, contribution, method, and evidence.
- Keep claims traceable and reviewer-facing.
- Use venue-specific output assets only after checking official instructions.

## Compared with Nature/CNS style

Nature/CNS-style writing is often more narrative and broad-audience. IEEE journal writing is usually more technical, section-driven, and evidence-dense.

## Compared with Elsevier

Do not assume Elsevier requirements transfer to IEEE. Templates, submission assets, keywords/index terms, highlights, declarations, and reference styles can differ substantially.

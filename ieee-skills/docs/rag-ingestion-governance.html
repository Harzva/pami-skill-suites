RAG Ingestion Governance

Release: v2.5.0

This page explains the three RAG modes used by IEEE Skills and the standalone main-figure corpus.

  • metadata-only RAG: enabled for seed records only.
  • image-embedding RAG: blocked until permission evidence and human approval pass.
  • public-gallery RAG: blocked until public display rights and attribution requirements pass.
Do not ingest images, captions, labels, or layouts for reuse from Sci-Hub, pirated PDFs, paywalled-only PDFs, copyright-unknown PDFs, or unauthorized sources.

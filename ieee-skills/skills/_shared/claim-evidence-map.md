# Claim-Evidence Map

Use this protocol for every rewrite or audit.

| Claim strength | Required support | Safer wording when support is weak |
|---|---|---|
| Strong superiority claim | Direct comparison under fair protocol | "shows competitive performance" or "improves under the tested setting" |
| Novelty claim | Clear relation to prior work | "to our knowledge" only with verified literature coverage |
| Generalization claim | External datasets / robustness tests | "in the evaluated datasets" |
| Deployment claim | Real-world validation / constraints | "suggests potential for" |
| Causal claim | Causal design or strong theory | "is associated with" or "may contribute to" |

Never create evidence that the user did not supply.

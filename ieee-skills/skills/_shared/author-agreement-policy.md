# Author Agreement and Rights Policy

This repository is not legal advice. It provides a verification checklist for authors preparing IEEE journal submissions.

## Rules for every skill

- Do not invent author-agreement, copyright, open-access, APC, or license terms.
- Do not state that an author signed, selected, paid, or obtained permission unless the user provides it.
- Treat agreement and rights pages as live official sources that must be verified at post-acceptance or before final submission.
- Distinguish manuscript-writing tasks from legal/publishing-rights decisions.
- For reused figures, tables, text, datasets, or code, ask the author to verify license and permission status.

## Output pattern

Return policy-sensitive language as:

```text
[Author to verify on official page / submission portal: ...]
```

rather than making a false compliance claim.

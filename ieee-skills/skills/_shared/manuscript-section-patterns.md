# Manuscript Section Patterns for IEEE

## Abstract

Compress problem, gap, method, evidence, and conclusion without unsupported hype.

## Introduction

Move from field problem to precise gap to explicit contribution. End with a contribution list that can be defended by experiments or analysis.

## Related Work

Organize by technical families. Compare mechanisms and assumptions, not just topics.

## Method

Define notation, assumptions, algorithm steps, implementation details, complexity, and design motivation.

## Experiments

Each table or figure should answer a reviewer question. Include baselines, settings, ablations, robustness, and limitations.

## Conclusion

Restate what was demonstrated, not what is hoped. Keep future work specific.

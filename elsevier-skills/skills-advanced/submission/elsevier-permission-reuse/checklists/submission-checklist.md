# Submission Checklist: Elsevier Permission Reuse

- [ ] Component matches the target journal's article type and scope.
- [ ] Claims are traceable to evidence, citations, theory, or limitations.
- [ ] Terms match title, abstract, keywords/index terms, figures, tables, and conclusion.
- [ ] Figures/tables/captions are cited in order if present.
- [ ] Policy-sensitive statements are verified against official sources.
- [ ] No fabricated citations, experiments, author declarations, or permission claims.

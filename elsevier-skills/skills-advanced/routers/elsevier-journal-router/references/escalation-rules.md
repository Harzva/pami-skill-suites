# Escalation rules

Escalate to official-source checks when the task touches:

- author agreement, copyright, license, open access, APCs, permissions, or reuse;
- AI-use disclosure, ethics, competing interests, funding, or data availability;
- journal-specific submission portal requirements;
- final production files, proofs, or accepted manuscript rights;
- preprints, prior publication, conference extension, or simultaneous submission.

# Author Agreement Notes: Knowledge-Based Systems

Elsevier publishing agreement and license decisions are usually handled after acceptance through the corresponding author workflow. Journal guides also include sections on publishing agreement, license options, open access, permissions, and responsible sharing.

## Checks for this skill

- Verify the current ScienceDirect / Elsevier Guide for Authors for this journal.
- Verify Elsevier copyright policy and open-access license options.
- Verify whether the journal is subscription, hybrid, or fully open access.
- Verify funder-mandated license requirements before selecting a license.
- Verify third-party permissions for reused figures, tables, datasets, graphical abstracts, or text.
- Never state that an agreement has been signed, a license selected, or rights cleared unless the author provides that information.

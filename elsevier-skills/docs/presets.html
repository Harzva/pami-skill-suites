Why presets?

Presets are optional routing profiles for disciplines such as computer vision, medical AI, remote sensing, multimedia, data mining, and signal processing. They help the suite pick journal, component, submission, paper-mining, and evaluation modules without adding new default top-level skills.

Install policy

Preset routers live under presets/ and are distributed as dist/preset-*.zip or dist/presets.zip. They are not placed in skills/, so they do not increase default skill listing context.

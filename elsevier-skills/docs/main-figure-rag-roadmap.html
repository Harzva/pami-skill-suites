Main-Figure Website + Multimodal RAG Roadmap

Future v2.0.0 target: a separate main-figure website and multimodal research-figure RAG knowledge base with at least 100 legally sourced OA papers.

Planned data model

  • paper metadata and license
  • main figure image/crop
  • caption and safe paraphrase
  • visual rhetoric tags
  • section linkage: motivation, method, experiment, limitations
  • embedding-ready description, not copied text

Non-negotiable safety

Only lawful OA or permission-compatible assets. The RAG corpus should store structural descriptions and metadata; direct reuse of visuals requires license and attribution checks.

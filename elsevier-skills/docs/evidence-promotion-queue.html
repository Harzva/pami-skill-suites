Evidence Promotion Queue

v2.4.0 adds an evidence-gated promotion queue for OA main-figure candidates.

  • Data: main_figure_site/data/evidence_promotion_queue.json or data/evidence_promotion_queue.json.
  • Default gate: closed.
  • No new extraction is allowed until DOI, OA source, license, permission, third-party-material, and human-review evidence pass.

Forbidden sources remain: Sci-Hub, pirated PDFs, paywalled-only PDFs, copyright-unknown PDFs, and unauthorized image reuse.

Elsevier Visual Query Benchmark

Defines metadata-only scientific-figure retrieval queries, expected tags, expected seed records, and output constraints.

v2.6.0 policy

Only metadata-only seed records are eligible for RAG. Image-embedding and public-gallery modes remain blocked until DOI/OA/license evidence, permission evidence, third-party-material review, and human reviewer approval pass.

Safety

Do not use Sci-Hub, pirated PDFs, paywalled-only PDFs, copyright-unknown PDFs, or unauthorized image reuse. Retrieval outputs must provide safe summaries and structural lessons only.

# Reviewer Risk Taxonomy

## High-risk categories

- Venue or scope mismatch.
- Incremental novelty.
- Unsupported SOTA or superiority claim.
- Missing baselines or unfair baseline implementation.
- Missing ablations for claimed component contributions.
- Incomplete reproducibility details.
- Weak statistical support.
- Unclear ethical approval, privacy, licensing, or data-access status.
- Formatting or submission-package assumptions not checked against official instructions.

## Output rule

Always rank risks by expected review damage and propose the smallest credible fix first.

# Elsevier Manuscript Readiness Rubric

Score each item 0–2.

| Criterion | 0 | 1 | 2 |
|---|---|---|---|
| Journal fit | unclear | plausible | explicit and strong |
| Abstract | vague | acceptable | clear problem-method-result-conclusion |
| Highlights | missing/generic | present | finding-oriented and searchable |
| Keywords | generic | acceptable | targeted and discoverable |
| Method detail | insufficient | mostly clear | reproducible and well motivated |
| Experiments | weak | adequate | comprehensive and fair |
| Discussion | thin | adequate | interprets results and limitations |
| Package compliance | missing | partial | Guide-for-Authors-ready |

Interpretation:

- 0–7: high rejection risk.
- 8–12: revise before submission.
- 13–16: plausible submission-ready draft.

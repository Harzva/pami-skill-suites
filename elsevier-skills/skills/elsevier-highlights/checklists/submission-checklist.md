# Submission Checklist

## Scientific readiness

- [ ] Problem, gap, and contribution are explicit.
- [ ] Novelty is clearly separated from prior work.
- [ ] Each strong claim has data, analysis, citation, or limitation language.
- [ ] Baselines are fair and sufficiently described.
- [ ] Ablations test the central contribution.
- [ ] Failure cases and limitations are honest.
- [ ] Reproducibility details are sufficient for expert readers.

## Family-specific readiness

- [ ] Abstract and keywords are aligned with title and contribution.
- [ ] Highlights are prepared when required by the target journal.
- [ ] Graphical abstract is prepared when required or useful.
- [ ] Elsevier Guide for Authors, reference style, and source package requirements have been checked.
- [ ] Declarations, ethics, funding, competing interests, acknowledgements, and data availability statements are prepared when required.

## Integrity readiness

- [ ] No fabricated citations, results, reviewer claims, or editorial policies.
- [ ] All human/animal/medical/privacy/security issues are represented honestly when relevant.
- [ ] AI-assisted drafting/editing is handled according to current official policy and author responsibility.

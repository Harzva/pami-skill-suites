# Motivation Organization

Publisher family: Elsevier

Move from real limitation to technical gap to method necessity without hype.

## Fixed routines

### Pain-point-to-gap

**Sequence:** application/research pain → why existing methods fail → specific missing mechanism → proposed design principle

**Reviewer risk:** Generic motivation sounds like introduction boilerplate.

### Toy contrast figure

**Sequence:** old paradigm → observed failure → new insight → resulting architecture/algorithm

**Reviewer risk:** A motivation figure without contrast becomes decorative.

### Gap taxonomy

**Sequence:** what is solved → what remains unsolved → where this paper sits → what it deliberately does not solve

**Reviewer risk:** Reviewers may say novelty is incremental if gap is vague.

## Output contract

- motivation ladder
- gap table
- main/motivation figure brief
- overclaim guardrails

## Safety note
These routines are structural best-practice patterns, not official publisher rules and not text to copy from template papers.
